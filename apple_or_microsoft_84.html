Microsoft's $15B hedge against the dollar: Analyst
What's behind Microsoft's big beat?
To combat the strong dollar, Microsoft (NASDAQ: MSFT) engaged in about $15 billion in currency hedging in the latest quarter, longtime Microsoft analyst Rick Sherlund said Friday.
In its earnings statement, Microsoft pointed to increasing usage of its enterprise cloud service Azure, cloud-based Office 365, search engine Bing and Xbox Live gaming.
Going forward, the Nomura analyst sees tough comparisons for Microsoft in the next two quarters.
