SAN FRANCISCO, April 24 (Reuters) - Software developers who tried on an Apple Watch for the first time on Friday predicted a rush of new apps over the next few months, particularly in areas including health and messaging.
"Health, transportation and social media will be the three main industries that will utilize the Apple Watch."
Tracking firm App Annie counted 3,061 total apps supporting Apple Watch on Friday.
