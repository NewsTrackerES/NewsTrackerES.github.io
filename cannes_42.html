CANNES WATCH: Portman says new Israeli isn't political
CANNES, France (AP) - Natalie Portman's directorial debut, "A Tale of Love and Darkness," is set around the time of the formation of Israel, but the Oscar-winning actress says it doesn't have a political agenda.
The film, adapted from an autobiographical novel by Amos Oz, premiered over the weekend at the Cannes Film Festival.
