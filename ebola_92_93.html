Trial begins in Guinea for suspects accused of killing journalists and Ebola workers
CONAKRY, Guinea - The trial of people accused of killing eight Ebola health workers and journalists opened Monday in a remote region of Guinea.
The killings happened when a delegation of health care workers, including top health officials from the nearby town, visited Womey last September to raise awareness about how to combat Ebola.
