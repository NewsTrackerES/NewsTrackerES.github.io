NEW YORK (AP) - Facebook is trying to clarify what posts, images and other content it allows on its site and why.
Facebook users who believe that a particular page or content violates the site's standards can click a "report" link to notify Facebook.
Facebook restricts content in countries where it violates local laws, even if that content does not violate its community standards.
