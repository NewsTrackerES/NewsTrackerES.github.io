The natural order of the universe was disrupted recently when BuzzFeed, NBC News, The New York Times and a number of other media companies ceded to Facebook the marketing and monetization of portions of their valuable content.
In addition to the trio mentioned above, the other media companies that will be funneling content to Facebook are The Atlantic, BBC News, Bild, The Guardian, National Geographic and Spiegel Online.
According to Alexa.com, the average user spends 18.4 minutes per day on Facebook, compared with 9.5 minutes at The New York Times, 6.4 minutes at NBC News and 5.4 minutes at BuzzFeed.
If Facebook sees that someone likes cooking Italian food, it can slip relevant recipes from The New York Times food page into that user's news feed, paired conveniently with an ad for a pasta maker.
