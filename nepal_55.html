A Canberra nurse and firefighter say they are devastated to learn their life's work - a charity-funded health clinic in the Langtang Valley in Nepal - has been crushed and staff likely killed in the earthquake.
ACT firefighter and Queanbeyan resident Geoff Bartram, one of Langtang Valley Health charity's trustees, left on Friday for Nepal to conduct a scoping study to find how best they can help.
