A court in southern Nepal has sentenced a man to 35 years in prison for the rape and murder of a 7-year-old girl, officials said Wednesday, in a case that sparked outrage in the Himalayan nation.
The sentences are the maximum allowed for those offenses in Nepal.
Nepal does not have capital punishment.
There are conflicting data on sexual crimes in Nepal, but rights groups say the number of rape cases in the country has increased in recent years.
