MAIDUGURI, Nigeria (AP) - Nigeria's home-grown Boko Haram group, newly weakened by a multinational force that has dislodged it from a score of northeastern towns, reportedly pledged formal allegiance to the Islamic State group.
Boko Haram in August followed the lead of IS in declaring an Islamic caliphate in northeast Nigeria that grew to cover an area the size of Belgium.
The Islamic State had declared a caliphate in vast swaths of territory that it controls in Iraq and Syria.
