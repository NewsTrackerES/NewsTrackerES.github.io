R&B singer The Weeknd, in sultry mood, performs during SXSW
The R&B singer performed Thursday on the second night of the "YouTube at SXSW" event at the Coppertank Events Center.
"Can I ask you something, Austin, Texas? I want to ask you a question, SXSW. Can I get sexy for you, SXSW?" he asked the feverish crowd of a few hundred.
"YouTube at SXSW" is a pre-party for Monday's YouTube Music Awards.
