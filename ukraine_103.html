Ukraine, in snub to Moscow, to adopt British war-time symbol, ditch Soviet war name
Ukraine will then mark Victory day on May 9 with its own war veterans' march in Kiev and several other big cities.
The move is part of broader changes undertaken by the pro-Western leadership to rid Ukraine of vestiges of Soviet rule following the conflict in the east in which more than 6,100 people have been killed.
In the east of Ukraine, a fragile ceasefire declared in February is holding, although the Kiev military said the situation remained tense near Mariupol, a coastal city on the Sea of Azov.
