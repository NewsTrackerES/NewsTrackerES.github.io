KIEV (Reuters) - Ukraine has signed a new agreement to buy Russian gas over the next three months at $248 per thousand cubic meters, the Ukrainian energy ministry said in a statement on Thursday.
Apart from the price, the agreement extended all the other terms of the 'winter package' which has just lapsed between Ukraine's state gas concern Naftogaz and Russia's gas giant Gazprom (GAZP.MM), the statement said.

Previous gas disputes between Ukraine and Russia have affected European Union markets, where Gazprom covers a third of gas demand.
Around 40 percent of that gas travels via Ukraine.
