The 9th U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals in Seattle said a lower court judge misapplied the law in finding that Xbox owners in the United States could not sue for damages as a group.
Microsoft has sold tens of millions of Xbox 360 consoles since their Nov. 2005 launch.
Microsoft countered that class certification was improper because just 0.4 % of Xbox owners reported disc scratches, and that misuse was the cause.
